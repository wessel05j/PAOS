# PAOS — Personal AI Operating System
PAOS (Personal AI Operating System) is an open-source platform designed to help individuals build and manage their own AI-powered systems.
The vision of PAOS is to give every person the ability to create their own AI "empire" — a structured system of AI agents, tools, and workflows that help turn ideas into reality.
Instead of being limited to a single AI assistant, PAOS allows users to design their own AI organization.

## Core Philosophy
PAOS is built around several core principles:
- **Human-first control** — The user is always in control of their system.
- **Modular AI agents** — AI capabilities are structured into specialized agents.
- **Safe baseline** — The default system is secure and controlled just like any other OS.
- **Expandable architecture** — Users can grow their AI system over time.
- **Open ecosystem** — PAOS is open-source and designed to evolve with community contributions.

## Current Status
PAOS is currently in the **vision and architecture design phase**.
Development will begin once the baseline architecture and principles are clearly defined.
